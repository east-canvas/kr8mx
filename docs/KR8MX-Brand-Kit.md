## 1. Brand Essence

- **One line:** Performance and precision, elevated to a single higher standard.
- **Positioning:** A premium, clinical-clean performance brand, not a loud "gas-station" energy label. Restraint, engineering, and a flavor-coded system are the whole personality.
- **Two product lines, one design language:**
  - **Performance Line**, *Energy Drinks*. Tagline: **PERFORMANCE. ELEVATED.**
  - **Precision Line**, *Tablets* (bottles + blisters). Tagline: **PRECISION. ELEVATED.**
- **Brand word:** **ELEVATED.** (always with the full stop when used as a lockup.)
- **Personality:** Sharp · Controlled · Composed · Precise · Premium · Athletic. Never hype, never medicinal, never cute.
- **The formula, MitraGen+™:** MitraGen+™ is a proprietary formulation that KR8MX products are **built with**, the premium formula at the core of the line and the reason it reads premium. Treat MitraGen+™ as a separate, independently-owned formulation: always describe KR8MX as *built with* or *powered by* MitraGen+™, and never as its owner, creator, or "our" blend. Always write it with the `+` and the `™`.

## 2. Logo System

### The wordmark
**KR8MX**, a custom, locked logotype (not a system font). Five uppercase characters in one row: **K · R · 8 · M · X**.
- Bold, geometric, faceted sans with **flat chiseled terminals and sharp mitered corners** (no rounding, no serifs).
- The **8** is a squared/hexagonal stacked figure-eight with cut corners, never a plain rounded numeral.
- The final **X is the hero device:** an **asymmetric X** made of **two curved, tapering blade/fin shapes in rotational symmetry, bisected by one thin elongated diagonal slash** that extends *above the cap line and below the baseline*. Read it as an X that has been "cut" by a fine diagonal.

### The X device (standalone monogram)
The slashed-X on its own: two curved tapering blades crossed by the thin diagonal line. Use as app icon, favicon, seal, watermark, and pattern motif.

### Lockups & orientation
- **Horizontal wordmark**, default (labels, web header, most uses).
- **Vertical wordmark**, the letters stacked top-to-bottom, slashed-X at top; used on tall formats (drink cans).
- Keep the slashed-X's proportions and the diagonal's overshoot intact in every lockup.

### Color variants
- **Ink wordmark** (near-black `#1E2528`) on white / silver / metallic surfaces, e.g., the drink can.
- **White wordmark** on saturated flavor-color fields, e.g., tablet labels & blisters.
- One-color only. No gradients inside the letterforms.

### Clear space & minimums
- Clear space = the height of the "8" on all sides.
- Min width: 22 mm print / 96 px screen (horizontal). Below that, use the X device alone.

### Don'ts
Don't recolor to a flavor hue · don't outline, emboss, add shadows, or 3D · don't stretch/condense · don't rotate the wordmark · don't fill the letterforms with pattern · don't reconstruct the X as a plain two-stroke X.

## 3. Color System

The system is **white-forward with a single saturated flavor accent per SKU** and near-black ink for type. Neutrals carry the layout; flavor color is the one bold move.

### 3.1 Core neutrals (locked, from the live site tokens)

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Ink / Graphite | `#1E2528` | Primary text, ink wordmark, hairline dark |
| True Obsidian | `#0B0B0D` | Max-contrast ink (can typography, deep shadow) |
| Soft White | `#FAFBFC` | Page / canvas background |
| Surface White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, labels, panels |
| Mist Gray | `#EEF1F4` | Raised surfaces, fills |
| Satin Silver | `#DDE3E8` | Hairlines, dividers, borders |
| Text Secondary | `#48545B` | Body secondary |
| Text Muted | `#8B969D` | Captions, meta |
| Platinum | `#E5E7EA` | Metallic can highlight |
| Silver | `#A7AAAD` | Metallic can midtone |

### 3.2 Flavor accents, the 5-flavor system (both lines share the same five)

Each flavor has an **Accent** (locked site/UI token, use for web tints, gradients, chips), a **Core** (saturated packaging fill), and a **Deep** (gradient/shadow end). Core/Deep are matched to the current packaging and should be pinned to Pantone/print values before a print run.

| Flavor | Accent (locked) | Core (packaging) | Deep (gradient end) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Grape** | `#8E6CCF` | `#7A3FB0` | `#4C2A7E` |
| **Strawberry** | `#EB4C5B` | `#E12E40` | `#A5182A` |
| **Blue Razz** | `#20B1C9` | `#12A2C0` | `#0B6E85` |
| **Lemon** | `#FFD34D` | `#F5C518` | `#CE9E00` |
| **Peach** | `#FF9A4D` | `#F5822E` | `#D2611A` |

**Rules**
- One flavor color per composition. Never mix two flavor hues in a single SKU's artwork.
- Flavor gradients run **Core → Deep**, diagonally or top-light. Keep them subtle on white; rich on the label fill.
- On white/light layouts, use the **Accent** for small elements and the **Core** for bold fills/chips.
- Text stays ink or white, flavor color is for accents, chips, ribbons, and label fields, not body copy.
- The full 5-flavor rainbow is only shown together in **range/lineup** shots.

### 3.3 Legacy / optional "night" mode
A dark treatment (Obsidian `#0B0B0D`, Carbon `#161618`, with an **Acid-Lime `#C6FF00`** scalpel accent) exists from the earlier performance concept. It is **optional/secondary**, use only for special dark editorial or motion moments. The canonical brand is the light system above; do not introduce acid-lime into standard packaging or the main site.

## 4. Typography

| Role | Typeface | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Logo** | KR8MX custom logotype | Locked artwork, never re-typed |
| **Display / Headlines** | **Archivo** (Black / Expanded, heavy) | Wide tracking, uppercase for impact |
| **Body / UI** | **Inter** | Clean, neutral, generous leading |
| **Pack callouts** (e.g., "10 TABS", "100MG") | Heavy condensed/geometric sans (Archivo Black or equivalent) | Tight, punchy, all-caps |

**Type behavior**
- Headlines: uppercase, **wide letter-spacing** (`0.06em` display, up to `0.28em` for kickers/eyebrows), tight leading (~1.02–1.15).
- Body: sentence case, relaxed leading (~1.6–1.75).
- Kicker/eyebrow labels: small, uppercase, `0.28em` tracking, muted or flavor-accent color.
- Restraint over volume, few sizes, lots of air.

**Type scale (px):** 11 · 12 · 14 · 16 · 18 · 22 · 28 · 36 · 48 · 64 · 88.
**Tracking tokens:** display `0.06em` · kicker `0.28em` · wide `0.02em`.

## 5. Graphic Language

- **Halftone dot ribbons**, dotted, DNA-helix/ribbon swooshes in the flavor Core color; the signature texture on the **drink cans** (mirrored left/right of the wordmark).
- **Line-wave / topographic contours**, fine flowing contour lines behind the label content on **tablets** (bottles + blisters). Flavor-colored, low contrast.
- **Slashed-X motif**, the X device as a large faint watermark, a pattern tile, or a small brand accent.
- **Halftone dot field**, subtle small-dot gradient fields as neutral backgrounds in studio/marketing layouts.
- **Condensation / droplets**, realistic water beading on drink cans only (freshness cue). Not used on tablets.
- **Hairlines & chips**, thin `1px` satin-silver rules; flavor "chips" are pill/parallelogram tags holding the flavor name (e.g., `/// BLUE RAZZ`).

**Corner radii:** 2 / 4 / 8 / 14 px. **Motion:** subtle only, 150–250 ms, `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` ease-out. No bounce, no spin.

## 6. Product Formats & Label Anatomy

**Flavors (all lines):** Grape · Strawberry · Blue Razz · Lemon · Peach.

### 6.1 Energy Drink, can (Performance Line)
- Slim **12 FL OZ (355 mL)** can, **white/silver metallic** body with condensation, flavor halftone ribbons.
- Vertical anatomy, top→bottom: **PERFORMANCE. ELEVATED.** (kicker, flavor accent) → **slashed-X** → **vertical KR8MX wordmark** (ink/black) → **MITRAGEN+ / PROPRIETARY SIGNATURE+** (flavor accent) → **flavor chip** (`/// PEACH`) → **ENERGY DRINK** → **12 FL OZ (355 ML)**.
- Wordmark is **ink/black** here (dark on metallic white).

### 6.2 Tablets, bottle (Precision Line)
- Clear PET bottle, **white textured child-resistant cap**, **flavor-gradient wrap label** (Core→Deep).
- Anatomy: **NOT FOR SALE TO PERSONS UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE.** → **horizontal KR8MX** (white) → black **"10 TABS"** pill → **100MG MitraGen+™ PER TAB** → **state compliance seal** (e.g., "Florida Compliant") → **flavor chip** (white pill, flavor name) → servings line: **2 SERVINGS PER TABLET | 150MG PER SERVING · 20 TOTAL SERVINGS | 10 TABLETS PER BOTTLE** → **Net Wt 4.24oz (120.20g)**.
- Wordmark is **white** on the colored label.

> **Dosage schema (confirmed):** serving size = **½ tablet**. **150 mg per serving** → **300 mg per tablet** (2 servings/tablet). The **100 mg MitraGen+™ per tab** callout is the *active* proprietary-blend amount, a different measure from the 300 mg per-tablet serving amount, so label the two distinctly (active vs. per-tablet) to avoid reading as a contradiction.
> - **10-tab bottle** → 20 servings · 3,000 mg total (150 mg × 20).
> - **5-tab blister** → 10 servings · 1,500 mg total.
> - Keep these figures identical across bottle, blister, and web.

### 6.3 Tablets, blister card (Precision Line)
- Hang-hole card, **flavor-color header** with line-wave graphic, **21+** badge top corner.
- **KR8MX wordmark** (white) → black **"5 TABS"** pill → **100MG MitraGen+™ PER TAB** → **five tablets in a quincunx "X" arrangement** (flavor-colored, **scored** tablets, the score line supports the ½-tablet serving) → **flavor name** at the base.

## 7. Photography & Art Direction (lighter system)

- **Bright studio, white-forward.** Seamless white, **soft marble surfaces**, gentle reflections, halftone-dot gradient backgrounds.
- Even, soft lighting; crisp product focus; generous negative space.
- **Lineup/range** shots: all five flavors in a row or grid, evenly lit, reflections on marble.
- Cans read cold/fresh (condensation); tablets read clinical/clean (no droplets).
- Avoid: dark grungy backdrops, neon glow, lifestyle clutter, lens flare, busy props. Premium and quiet.

## 8. Voice & Copy

- **Tone:** confident, minimal, precise. Short declaratives. Editorial restraint.
- **Signature lines:** "Performance. Elevated." · "Precision. Elevated." · "Sharp. Controlled. Elevated." · "Five flavors. One standard."
- **Approved vocabulary:** elevated, performance, precision, sharp, controlled, composed, clean, crisp, standard, proprietary, refined, flavor-coded, dialed in.
- **Sensory (flavor) language is encouraged**, e.g., "sun-ripe peach, clean to the finish."
- **No em dashes, ever (hard rule).** Never use "—" in any copy. Use commas, periods, or colons instead. Copy must read organically for SEO. This applies to every headline, body line, caption, alt text, and title.

### 8.1 Compliance, mandatory & prohibited (read carefully)
KR8MX is a **kratom-derived** product. Copy may **not** make structure/function, health, or effect claims.

- **Prohibited claim words** (never in marketing copy): `energy*`, `focus`, `boost`, `enhance`, `mood`, `relief`, `calm`, `treat`, `cure`, `dose`, `wellness`. *(\*"Energy Drink" is the ONE whitelisted use, it is the legal category descriptor on drink packaging only.)*
- **Required elements** on packaging: **21+ / "NOT FOR SALE TO PERSONS UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE"**, MitraGen+™ + per-tab mg, servings, **net weight**, and the applicable **state compliance seal**.
- No dosage or usage guidance in marketing. No implied benefits, effects, or comparisons to controlled substances.
- The FDA disclaimer ("not intended to diagnose, treat, cure…") lives only in legal/disclaimer copy, never as a marketing headline.
